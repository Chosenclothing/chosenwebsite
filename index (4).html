Vision Board

Collection Ideas

Here are some directions we could grow into — tag us on Instagram and let us know which one speaks to you!

🏛 The Armor Drop

Inspired by Ephesians 6 — the Full Armor of God. Each piece (belt, breastplate, shield, helmet) becomes a garment in a matching set. Structured, bold, military-adjacent aesthetic.

📖 Verse Series

Rotating drops anchored to one power verse per season. Typography-led designs where the scripture itself becomes the visual statement. Clean and wearable for any occasion.

🌿 The Roots Collection

Heritage-inspired pieces that connect faith to Black church tradition, gospel music, and community. Rich earth tones, hand-lettered styles, deep cultural resonance.

🔥 Revival Streetwear

Fire and bold graphic tees that look like streetwear but carry a gospel message. Think Supreme meets the Great Commission. For the young believer who lives in the culture.

🕊 Still Small Voice

A minimalist, quiet luxury line. Subtle embossed crosses, tonal embroidery, linen and heavyweight cotton. Faith for those who prefer to let their life — not their shirt — do the talking.

🏆 Athletes of Faith

Performance and athletic gear for the believer who trains hard. Moisture-wicking tees, compression gear, and gym bags printed with Philippians 4:13. Built for champions.
